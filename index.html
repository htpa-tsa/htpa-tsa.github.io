About Us

Established at the height of the COVID-19 pandemic, the HTPA Technology Student Association provides students with plentiful exposure, resources, and support within STEM-based fields, with a focus on autodidacticism as an approach to fulfill personal interests. The club's participation in competitions throughout its initial year of inception brought much success in game development and cybersecurity, acquiring second place at the state level and finalist status at the national level, respectively.
